Project-2: Multi-Class Classification of Research Articles

Researchers have access to large online archives of scientific articles. As a consequence, finding relevant articles has become more and more difficult.
Tagging or topic modelling provides a way to give clear token of identification to research articles which facilitates recommendation and search process in a much better way.
Goal here is to predict the tags for each article based on the abstract words(NLP).
