Project-3: Retail Sales Forecast - Time Series

Time Series Analysis is very important when you want to build Forecast models such as Sales Forecast, etc.
A Time series is a series of data points indexed in time order.
It helps organizations understand the underlying causes of trends or systemic patterns over time.
Using data visualizations, business users can see seasonal trends and dig deeper into why these trends occur.
In this article we will discuss how a Time series model can be built which will help in forecasting the Sales of the Retail Organization for next 2 months.
